## About Me

I am Bhavya Shah, an AI/ML developer focused on building practical intelligent systems with Generative AI, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), voice agents, and computer vision. My work spans production-grade AI pipelines, LangChain-based retrieval systems, LLM evaluation and fine-tuning workflows, and OpenCV-powered automation for real-world use cases in logistics, healthcare, and knowledge search.

I enjoy turning complex AI ideas into usable products, from voice-driven assistants and PDF search systems to healthcare image classification and port security solutions.
