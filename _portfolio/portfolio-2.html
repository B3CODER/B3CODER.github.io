--- title: "Revolutionizing Healthcare: Cutting-Edge Medical Image Classification" excerpt: "Classify images to identify specific types of skin or lung cancer and predict outcomes based on these categories.
" collection: portfolio --- Two Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) models were created to classify skin and lung cancer images, utilizing data from Kaggle datasets. Information about cancer types and treatments was gathered through web scraping. The user-friendly frontend was developed using Flask and hosted on a local server, allowing users to classify cancer images and access valuable information about cancer types and potential treatments.
